Reason:

The Lamborghini Urus is ranked 3rd in "My Top 5 Dream Cars" because it is the kind of car that hits both sides of what I value—living my life to the fullest and owning something that feels like a serious piece of automotive history. On the lifestyle side, it’s perfect because it’s not just a supercar flex; it’s an SUV that I could actually use every day, whether that’s taking friends out, going on trips, or just enjoying the comfort while still having insane speed and power under the hood. At the same time, it’s a Lamborghini, which makes it a collector’s dream. It’s rare, it holds its value, and it represents a turning point in car culture where exotic brands started making performance SUVs.
