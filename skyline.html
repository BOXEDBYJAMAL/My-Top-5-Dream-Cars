Reason:

The Nissan Skyline GT-R is ranked first in "My Top 5 Dream Cars" because it is far beyond its role in 2 Fast 2 Furious, but because it represents the topmost of Japanese performance engineering and car culture. Powered by the legendary RB26DETT twin-turbo inline-six engine and equipped with Nissan’s advanced ATTESA all-wheel-drive system, the skyline offered exceptional handling and tuning potential that made it a favorite among enthusiasts and racers alike. Its bold, boxy design and signature Bayside Blue paint gave it a design that remains unforgettable to me, while its rarity—especially in markets like the United States where it was long considered“forbidden fruit”—has elevated it to a highly collectible status. It's more than just a movie car, the Skyline GT-R embodies speed, innovation, and cultural prestige, making it one of the most iconic and desirable sports cars in automotive history.
